The Project Concept

CricScore : Track the Action of Your Hometown Cricket Heroes Stay connected to every match with CricScore! Get real-time updates, ball-by-ball and stats for your favorite local teams. Easily manage team rosters, track performances, and relive game highlights—all in one place. Support your local cricket stars with CricScore! With Cric Score, you can create and manage teams effortlessly. Start by adding teams with unique names to represent each squad. Dive deeper by setting up team rosters that feature your favorite team.
